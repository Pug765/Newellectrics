Our embedded system

Our embedded system is a light sensitive system, When the brightness levels drop below a certain point, a light turns on.

How we made this

We made this embedded system by connecting a microbit to a laptop and connecting it to a breadboard. We then connected 1 jumper cable from the 30 on the blue line to OV. Connected 1 jumper cable from the 25 on the red line to 3V. Connected resistor from 18 blue to 12 g And connected last jumper cable
